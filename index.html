What we do

Paris has countless of attractions and destinations that visitors can choose from, however, picking which one to visit can be tough and overwhelming especially if you are on a tight schedule and only have a few days to stay. We here at Paris Smart Tours can help you navigate through the ins and outs of Paris, which destinations to visit and to avoid at which times, which restaurants to dine-in and which ones aren't worth a dime, which souvenirs are worth buying and which ones are scam.
